Man Finally Step on the Moon!!

Apollo 11

Apollo 11 was a result of hard work of many scientists working in NASA. This mission happened in 1969. Its most important feat was that man would finally step on the moon.This was the first of missions to put a man on the moon, it had also set a record of the first man on the moon. Its crew was Micheal Collins, Buzz Aldrin, and Neil Armstrong (first man on the moon).
